🛡️ Cybersecurity Basics

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access or attacks. It’s a key part of modern life, from your phone to large enterprise networks.

🔐 Why It Matters

Everyone—students, developers, professionals—should understand the importance of cybersecurity. Ignoring it can lead to personal data leaks, business loss, and reputational damage.

🧰 Basic Cyber Hygiene

  • Use strong, unique passwords and a password manager
  • Keep your software and systems up to date
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
  • Be cautious of emails, links, and attachments
  • Back up your important data regularly

🚨 Common Threats

Cyberattacks come in many forms. Here are a few common ones:

  • Phishing: Fraudulent attempts to steal credentials through fake emails or websites.
  • Malware: Harmful software such as viruses, worms, or ransomware.
  • Social Engineering: Manipulating individuals into giving up sensitive information.

💡 Quick Tip:

Never reuse passwords across sites. If one gets compromised, others can be too.
